Valorant

Description:Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter where teams of agents battle it out in strategic, objective-based gameplay. Each agent brings unique abilities to the fight, blending precise gunplay with powerful skills. Players must plan their moves, execute tactics, and outsmart opponents to secure objectives and claim victory. With a focus on team coordination and tactical depth, Valorant delivers intense, skillful action in every match.
