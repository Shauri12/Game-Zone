GTA V

Description:Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game set in the sprawling city of Los Santos. Players control three distinct characters—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—each with their own storylines and skills. The game features a rich, immersive world where you can engage in missions, explore diverse environments, and participate in various activities like heists, races, and side quests. With its detailed graphics, expansive gameplay, and deep narrative, GTA V offers a dynamic and thrilling experience in both single-player and the expansive online multiplayer mode, GTA Online.
